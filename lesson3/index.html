The Purpose of This Site

The purpose of the website is to help promote business, tourism and people in the Star, Idaho area. We want to join together as a community to help strengthen one another. This is where we can gather as local business leaders and be unified. On this site you can learn more about Star and our history. You can also learn about the different businesses here, join the Chamber of Commerce and get in contact with us. Through the Chamber of Commerce we can help one another and be fortified.
