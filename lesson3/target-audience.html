The Target Audience

My target Audience for this page is males ages 40-60. They live in the Treasure Valley area and grew up there Idaho. They want to preserve the rich history of Idaho and help in any way they can to help their city. They graduated from college and now they own or share their business. They are middle to lower high class. This means they make around $70,000 - $150,000. They enjoy hanging out with their family. Going on trips and supporting their local businesses. My target Audience is looking for ways to help their community while growing their business.

Personas

Persona Number 1: Jeff Catcher

Jeff Catcher is a 46 year old man who lives in Star. He is a local dentist. He was born and raised in Boise, the capital of Idaho that is 35 minutes from the star. He graduated from BSU and then went to dental school in Washington. When he graduated from dental school he worked under another dentist in Middleton Idaho. He then realized the need for a dentist in star and opened his own practice. He wants to better help his town and wants to grow his business. He knows how important dentistry is and wants to help those in the community who need their dental work done. They have a friend who is a member of the chamber of commerce and so they are looking to get some more information about it. He googles about it using his computer while at work. He is looking to spend around 10 minutes to get all the information he needs. He was motivated by trying to help his business and city. He is looking into becoming a member and trying to decide who option might be best for him. He needs to learn more about the member options and how they will help.

Persona Number 2: Mariah Sonland

Mariah Sonland is a 26 year old female from Eugene, Oregon. She recently graduated from college with a degree in Speech Pathology. She was looking to move to Star to open up her own speech pathology center. She has an aunt who lives in Star and recommended her to move there. She worked for an elementary school in Oregon but wanted to do something more. She heard that Treasure Valley has been growing and thought that it could use a speech center. She wanted to learn more about the businesses and history of Star. She wanted to see if there is a need for a speech pathology center. She also wanted to learn more about all that star has so she can decide if it would be a good place to move to. She was looking to do some quick research so she went on her phone to look up the stars chamber of commerce. She will probably spend around 10-20 minutes learning more about Star. She is motivated to learn more about the community and all it has to offer. She is looking to see if there are other speech centers there or if it is something the community needs.
