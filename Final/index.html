About Carlsbad

Carlsbad is located in southern california and is San Diego County. Carlsbad sits on the Pacific Ocean and is located south of Oceanside.It was originally settled in the late 1860s by ranchers. In 1885 it started to turn into a vacation destination. Today the city is home to many beach front resorts, museums, flower fields and Legoland California. It is also known for its near perfect weather and golfing. Carlsbad has been known as one of the best places to live and/or retire. We are happy to call Carlsbad the home of our beautiful business. It is a great town to grow our fresh produce. We are grateful to be able to share our amazing resources with others.
